PAPER YARN TUBES ( TEXTILE PAPER TUBES )

Paper Tubes

YCE's Textile Paper Tubes are expertly crafted to meet all requirements for fabric and yarn winding. Made from premium recycled paperboard and produced with advanced machinery, these tubes are ideal for various applications including POY winding, DTY winding, FDY winding, open-end spinning, fabric and jute winding, non-woven fabric, carpet yarn winding, and more. Our manufacturing process uses starch-based adhesives, free from harmful chemicals, ensuring that our paper tubes are 100% recyclable.

FEATURES OF PAPER TUBES ( TEXTILE PAPER TUBES )

  • NOTCHES - V, Y, U notches are available as per customer's requirement.
  • BULL NOSE - Bull nose design is preferred as it allows the yarn to flow smoothly from the tubes and fits perfectly with the adaptors in yarn spinning machines.
  • PRINTING - Various colors are available for printing on both the inside and outside of the tubes, and we also offer dual outside printing for easy yarn identification.
  • GROOVES - Reserve Groove for yarn tail placement is available as well as other grooves for special needs.

The paper tubes can be printed with ink on the top nose and tapered ends in different colors and recognizable patterns, allowing for easy identification of yarns with various counts, simplifying handling for your employees and customers. Customer logos, unique symbols, or signs can also be printed on the cones for branding purposes.

BPC offers a wide range of colored parchment as the final layer on our textile tubes. This parchment paper provides high resistance to abrasion, heat, and grease.
