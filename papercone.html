GUARANTEED DIMENSIONAL ACCURACY

To achieve targeted efficiency, cone design must adhere to automated winding machine manufacturers’ dimensional tolerances. All Yarn Care textile yarn cones are manufactured and guaranteed to a tolerance of ±1mm in length and ±0.25mm in inside diameter at both the top and the bottom of the product.
